The AFRIFEST concert is a unique outdoor 2 day music EXTRAVAGANZA. Taking place at Streatham Common, where Africans and Non-Africans alike can njoy fusion of the best contemporary African music, Classical styles of Afrobeat, Afropop and Soukous, and much more. The new generation style of Grime and Rap will not be left out as it is served with twists that have been creatively entwined to make it uniquely the preserve of new acts.

OVER 2 MILLION AFRICANS LIVE IN THE UK!
We know a majority of us miss home. And a large percentage of us are unable to travel home due to financial constraints. AFRIFEST 2023 provides a platform for us to come and experience home away from home. Africans who have never been to their countries of origin also gets to experience African entertainment and culture first-hand. With the vibrancy of the music, the food lovers of Africa can come to enjoy the event and ambience of Africa. Non-Africansw who are curious and eager to learn more can also venture to sate their curiosity in real time.

Get your taste buds ready for a culinary adventure at Afrifest2023! Our food court will feature a variety of traditional African cuisines from different countries across the continent. Come and experience the flavors of Africa like never before!

Get ready to be dazzled by the vibrant and colorful African fashion at Afrifest2023! From traditional to contemporary, our fashion show will showcase the best of African style and design. Don't miss this opportunity to witness the diversity of African fashion firsthand!

Are you a lover of spoken words? Then Afrifest2023 is the place to be! Immerse yourself in the richness of African culture as we showcase some of the most talented spoken words artists from across the continent
